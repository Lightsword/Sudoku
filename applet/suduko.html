Suduko Solver Applet

    How to use the Applet:

  • Enter a Suduko puzzle below and press "Solve" to solve the puzzle.
  • Coming soon: Press "Generate" to create a new puzzle.
  • Press "Clear" to clear the puzzle and start over.
  • Press "Validate" to verify that the board contains a valid solution.

  • To load a sample puzzle, select the sample and press "Load Sample".
  • To load the solution of a sample puzzle, select the sample and press "Load Solution".
  • To compare the current board to the solution of a sample puzzle,
    select the sample and press "Compare to Solution".

  • To use a different solver algoritm, select the desired algorithm before pressing "Solve".

  • The current version of the applet is v1.2c. Please verify that this version of
    the applet is running below. If not, your browser may have cached an older version.

And here is a summary of the rules:
  • The playing board consists of 81 cells, arranged in 9 rows and nine columns.
  • Since the board measures 9x9 cells, it can also be grouped into 9 squares, each with 9 cells.
  • Each cell is a member of a row, a column, and a square.
  • When the puzzle is solved, each cell must have a digit in it. Valid digits are 1-9.
  • For each cell, its digit may not be repeated in any other cell in the same row, column, or square.
  • Puzzles may have many empty squares, in which you attempt to place the correct digit.
